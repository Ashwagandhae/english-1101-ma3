Atlanta Food Bank

What Can You Do?

With the Atlanta Community Food Bank, you have the power to stop hunger in your community. Volunteering at the ACFB is easy yet rewarding. As a volunteer, you can help organize and sort food items, create packages for families in need, or simply help spread the word about the ACFB and its purpose. Signing up is as simple as going to this website, selecting an opportunity, and registering your information. There are dozens of opportunities, meaning that volunteering can work directly in conjunction with your schedule. No experience is needed: tasks are simple and intuitive while remaining engaging, as you directly contribute to addressing hunger for thousands of people. Additionally, you can organize a food drive at your local school or organization and contribute food directly to the ACFB, saving food from being thrown away and giving it new purpose to those who need it most.
